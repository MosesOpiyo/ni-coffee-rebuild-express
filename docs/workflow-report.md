# NICE Coffee Platform Complete Workflow Report

## 1. User Roles & Responsibilities

### Farmer
**Access Level**: Basic
**Authentication**: Phone OTP
**Primary Functions**:
- Record harvests and deliveries
- Track payments and earnings
- View quality feedback
- Access training materials
- Communicate with cooperative

### Cooperative Manager
**Access Level**: Moderate
**Authentication**: Email + Password
**Primary Functions**:
- Manage farmer relationships
- Process deliveries and payments
- Quality control and grading
- Batch aggregation
- Training coordination

### Quality Officer
**Access Level**: Moderate
**Authentication**: Email + Password/Google OAuth
**Primary Functions**:
- Quality assessment
- Grading reports
- Training delivery
- Feedback provision
- Process monitoring

### Exporter
**Access Level**: Advanced
**Authentication**: Email + Password + MFA
**Primary Functions**:
- Logistics management
- Documentation handling
- Payment processing
- Compliance monitoring
- Buyer relationship management

### Importer
**Access Level**: Advanced
**Authentication**: Email + Password/Google OAuth
**Primary Functions**:
- Shipment tracking
- Quality verification
- Inventory management
- Payment processing
- Supplier relationship management
